Privacy Policy

Trip Tunes

Last updated: June 2026

1.Introduction

Trip Tunes is designed to help create personalized road-trip playlists while collecting as little personal information as possible.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Trip Tunes may process, what information is stored, and how that information is used.

By using Trip Tunes, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2.Information Collection and Use

Trip Tunes does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information in order to use the app.

The only personal information Trip Tunes may collect is an email address if you voluntarily choose to subscribe to the Trip Tunes mailing list.

Providing an email address is entirely optional and is not required to use any feature of the app.

Email addresses may be used to send occasional Trip Tunes updates, special playlists, product announcements, and information about other apps or products created by the developer.

3.Playlist Generation

When generating playlists, Trip Tunes processes information such as destinations, trip details, and music preferences in order to create personalized playlist recommendations.

This information is transmitted only as necessary to provide playlist-generation features and is not stored by Trip Tunes after processing is complete.

Trip Tunes does not maintain a database of user trips or listening preferences.

4.Shared Tapes

Trip Tunes allows users to create shareable tapes.

When a user chooses to share a tape, information necessary to recreate that shared experience may be stored so that the tape can be accessed by others using the share link.

Shared tapes are not associated with a user account, name, email address, or other identifying information.

5.Mailing List

Trip Tunes optionally allows users to subscribe to a mailing list by providing an email address.

Email addresses are used solely for mailing-list communications and are not linked to trips, playlists, shared tapes, or app activity.

Trip Tunes does not sell, rent, or share mailing-list information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Subscribers may unsubscribe at any time using the instructions included in mailing-list communications or by contacting the developer.

6.Data Storage

Trip Tunes generally operates without user accounts or persistent storage of personal information.

The app may store information locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account to support app functionality and synchronization across your devices.

Limited backend services are used only for features such as shared tapes and optional mailing-list subscriptions.

7.Third-Party Services

Trip Tunes relies on third-party services to provide certain functionality, including playlist generation, cloud synchronization, and app operation.

These services receive only the information necessary to perform their function on behalf of Trip Tunes.

8.Privacy by Design

Trip Tunes is intentionally designed to minimize data collection.

The app does not create user profiles, track users across services, or associate app activity with a personal identity.

Trip Tunes does not maintain accounts, and shared tapes are not connected to email addresses or other identifying information.

If a user subscribes to the mailing list, their email address is stored separately and is not connected to trips, playlists, shared tapes, or app activity.

9.Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information stored by Trip Tunes and its service providers. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10.Children's Privacy

Trip Tunes does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to Trip Tunes, please contact the developer so that the information can be removed.

11.Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this document.

Continued use of Trip Tunes after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

12.Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: triptunesapp@gmail.com

Thank you for using Trip Tunes!
